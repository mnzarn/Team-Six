About Us

Travel SU is a ridesharing platform made exclusively for Seattle University students. Our mission? Providing safe, reliable, and accessible transportation while fostering a sense of community on campus by hiring drivers from our very own campus. Need a ride to a nursing clinical, hiking trail, or even to a concert? Post a request! Planning to take a drive and want some company from an SU peer, or simply looking to make some extra cash by offering a ride? Post an ad and let others know how you'd like to be compensated. To join, simply log in with your SU credentials.
