What is TravelSU?

Welcome to TravelSU, your ultimate destination for student-friendly ridesharing in the city of Seattle! At TravelSU, we're not just about getting from point A to point B; we're about fostering connections, saving money, and sharing experiences.

Are you tired of overpriced rideshares draining your student budget? Look no further! TravelSU offers a unique solution that puts money back in your pocket. Whether you're offering a ride or hitching a lift, students can opt for free rides or simply chip in for gas, making travel affordable and accessible for everyone.

But we're more than just a ridesharing platform - we're a community. Connect with fellow students who share your interests and hobbies, turning your commute into an opportunity to make new friends and explore the city together. Whether you're a music lover, a foodie, or an outdoor enthusiast, TravelSU helps you find like-minded companions to enhance your journey.

Join the TravelSU community today and embark on affordable, enriching adventures across Seattle!

Manzi's Blurb


TravelSU is all about creating an easier way to ride-share when it comes to SeattleU students. Public transportation doesn't always go where you need to or the stops might become a nuisance. Ride-sharing apps have very high prices, especially in Seattle. With all this, it's hard to get to the places you need to as a student. With TravelSU, we hope to save students some money by providing rides to places other students may already be going. We also ensure the safety of all of our users.
